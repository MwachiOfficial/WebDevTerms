TCP/IP

TCP/IP is the conceptual model and set of communications protocols used on the Internet and similar computer networks. TCP is responsible for verifying the delivery of data and ensuring that packets arrive in the correct order. IP is responsible for routing those packets to the correct numerical address.

Role: It acts as the "backbone" of all internet communication, ensuring data sent from a sender reaches the receiver without corruption.
